How To Use This Yearbook!


This is the Home Page!

Welcome to the Virtual Yearbook! The home page shows all Senior profiles. Use the navbar at the top to explore the Virtual Yearbook's other pages, and click on "Belmont High School Yearbook" to come back to this page.

To sign someone's yearbook, click on their profile!


Sending A Message

Enter a message in the space provided below to compose a heartfelt note. Once you hit "Sign Yearbook", you will not be able to view what you sent. A checkmark (✓) will appear next to the recipient's name

If you would someone to sign your yearbook, click "Request Signing"!


My Messages!

Click on My Messages in the navbar to view the messages that people send you! Each person's messages will be displayed on a sticky note.

Scroll on the stickynote to view older messages or rest of a long heartfelt message!


Edit Profile!

Change your password, profile picture, quote, and future location in Edit Profile!

If your changes do not show up on the website, don't panic! The easist way to resolve this issue is log out and log back in.


My Requests!

View your yearbook signing requests here.

If someone requests, please make the effort to be a nice and compassionate person!


Teachers!

Show some love for the wonderful people who helped grow our minds!

Each teacher's bio is a thoughtful message for all their students, past and present, who are graduating this year. Click on their profile to send them a personal note of gratitude!


Map!

Want to see where your class is off to next year? Check out the map! Each location mark shows the name of the place and which people are going there.

Disclaimer: people with no listed plans will most likely be pursiuing their futures on dry land, not in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean.
